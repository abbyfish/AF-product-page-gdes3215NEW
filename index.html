grip

non-toxic ink

Time to let those bored-in-class doodles shine. The myth about ink staining your blood is long gone with our specially blended, safe ink designed to inspire all of those future tattoo artists.

recycled plastic

We care about the planet. That's why we encourage our users to write on recycled paper and to use the front AND the back! Our pens aren't the exception. We source our plastic straight from the recycling centers.

Our Story

It all started with one final exam thrown on 4 college students on one cold winter day at UVM. After a heated debate about what was the best study method, we figured out that using color coding was the common denominator. From there we talked with our product design and scientist friends and came up with the perfect pen that you see here today.
